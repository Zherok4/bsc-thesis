\section{Thesis outline}
The remainder of this thesis is structured as follows. Chapter~\ref{ch:background} provides the background necessary to understand the design decisions made throughout the thesis. It introduces the spreadsheet paradigm and its key concepts, the Cognitive Dimensions framework used to evaluate and motivate design choices, and an overview of related work in spreadsheet visualization. Chapter~\ref{ch:system-design} presents the design of the proposed tool. The system is divided into four layers: the presentation layer, the visualization layer, the parsing layer and the data layer.
