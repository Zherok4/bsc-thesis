\section*{Motivation}
Spreadsheets are prone to weak points known as Spreadsheet smells. Common smells are Feature Envy, where a formula accumulates excessive dependencies, and Inappropriate Intimacy, where data is tightly coupled across different worksheets \cite{hermans_detecting_2012}. (TODO: build reusability into it)
These smells hinder comprehension and make it harder for the users to find information. Readers of Spreadsheet spent 40\% of their time seeking for information, such as locating items, understanding interactions, tracing formula logic and understanding unfamiliar spreadsheet concepts \cite{srinivasa_ragavan_spreadsheet_2021}.
Improving Self-descriptiveness is therefore critical to reduce the time spent seeking for information. However visualization alone is insufficient. The lack of Bidirectionality forces users to constantly switch context on these information detours, further increasing the time and cognitive effort required.
