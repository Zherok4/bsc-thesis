\begin{abstract}
    Spreadsheet programming is one of the most used programming paradigm. Yet previous research identified that the current tools do not support the user in the comprehension process. Especially users spend a lot of time on information detours. This thesis presents a flow-graph based visualization tool to help the user understand and navigate spreadsheets without loosing their spatial context. To evaluate the usefulness an explorative study was conducted with 5 participants. In general the users found it helpful to understand formulas and intuitive to use. To correctly identify the benefits of the tool, future work has to conduct a more extensive study which also compares to traditional spreadsheet tools.
\end{abstract}
